Katy Perry and Other Celebs Reveal Their Favorite Candy

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, June 13, 2024

If it were up to Us, every day would be National Candy Day! Here are the sweet treats we think Katy Perry, Ryan Gosling and other stars may be reaching for to celebrate a day of indulgence on Friday, November 4.

Katy Perry

No mystery here! The “California Gurls” singer’s onetime Instagram bio? Saying she “Relates to ppl who choose juicy pear as their jelly belly flavor of choice.” She has also asked Twitter followers to retweet her if it’s their favorite, too.

Ashley Benson

When the Pretty Little Liars actress wants to satisfy her sweet tooth, she stops by Dylan’s Candy Bar to pick up Smarties, peach rings and strawberry puffs.

Jessica Biel

This year, the 7th Heaven alum might be indulging at her restaurant, Au Fudge, which serves desserts like a build-your-own ice cream sundae. But she’s also a Dylan’s Candy Bar devotee who has been spotted stocking up on coconut haystacks and Good ‘N Plenty licorice.

Ryan Gosling

The actor may not eat your cereal, but he’ll almost certainly accept your Hi Chews. He raved about the Japanese candy in a 2011 Esquire interview, saying, “Look at these! It’s the candy that never quits on you. This candy is always worth the price.”

Neil Patrick Harris

Chocolate — more specifically, Reese’s Peanut Butter Cups — speak to the triple threat’s soul. “There’s nothing better, it’s like an opiate,” he has gushed about the treats.

Chrissy Teigen

An omnivore through and through, the model and cookbook author told Us that she’s a sucker for “a regular Tootsie Roll and any kind of sour candy” in particular.

Leonardo DiCaprio

The actor satisfies his sweet tooth with non-GMO, fair trade UnReal chocolates. (They’re Patriots’ quarterback Tom Brady’s choice, too, even if his wife, model Gisele Bündchen, only lets their kids eat one Halloween candy each before tossing out the rest.)
