Jennifer Lopez Glows in Plunging Floral Gown at Daytime Beauty Awards

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, May 11, 2024

Jennifer Lopez got dressed to the nines to support her friend in a major way.

Yesterday at Los Angeles’s Taglyan Complex, the multi-hyphenate attended the fifth Daytime Beauty Awards, which honor science in the beauty industry, as well as professionals in the health and wellness spaces. She supported her friend fitness mogul Tracy Anderson at the event.

For the flashy occasion, Lopez took a glamorous route by wearing a white Bach Mai gown covered in a hot-pink watercolor floral print. The eye-popping dress featured a plunging V-neckline, a drop waist, and a flared puffy high-low skirt. She accessorized the look with more hot-pink elements, including a satin pink clutch by Rodo and and strappy heeled sandals with a pointed toe from Femme LA. She finished off her ensemble with delicate floral drop earrings.

Beauty-wise, the Marry Me star opted for a bold smoky eye paired with dark voluminous lashes and a dark pink lip. She swept her caramel hair up into a slicked-back bun, then further coordinated with a matching nude manicure and pedicure.

Posting photos of her final look on Instagram, Lopez wrote in the caption, “Congratulations Tracy 💕.”

Anderson responded in the comment section: “@jlo you are an exquisite human being! Your love in motion is completely heartening! Thank you for being there to support me! I have all of the respect in the world for you! 💓💓💓💓💓💓💓💓”

Lopez clearly has an affinity for fine, floral gowns. During a family vacation to Italy this past summer, she was spotted aboard a boat while zipped up in a white Zimmermann midi dress with a pastel pink and lavender watercolor floral print. The delicate piece additionally featured long matching straps at the waist, which Lopez tied off in a large, luscious bow. Lopez also wore a flowy floral dress on Mother’s Day—a lovely blue-and-white toile de Jouy print sundress with romantic puff sleeves.
