Instructions on how to reset the Sensi thermostat

To remove the thermostat face plate from the wall, gently pull on both sides of the thermostat face plate.

Remove the two AA batteries from the device and wait until the display screen turns blank before replacing them.

Replace the batteries and affix the face plate to the wall as you did before.

The thermostat should automatically reset after one minute.
