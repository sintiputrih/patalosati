Abantu abadumileyo bakhetha ukuba neMithombo yamanzi eKati ukugcina iikati zabo zanelisekile kwaye z

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, January 1, 0001

Ngaba ukhe wacinga malunga nokuba ubomi bakho bunokuba njani ngaphandle kobukho beekati zakho ezizithandayo? Nangona ubafuna ngamandla ebomini bakho, akunaxesha laneleyo lokuchitha nabo, ngakumbi phakathi evekini xa kufuneka usebenze ubusuku nemini. Oku kusebenza okufanayo kungekuphela kuwe kodwa nakubantu abadumileyo abaphila ubomi obuxakekileyo kwaye kufuneka bafuduke ngokukhawuleza kunokuba ucinga.

Namhlanje siza kuhlalutya umkhwa wokuthenga imithombo yamanzi ekati evela kubantu abadumileyo kwaye isasazeka kulo lonke uluntu. Wonke umntu angathanda ukubona kumajelo eendaba ezentlalo ukuba kwenzeka ntoni ngezilwanyana ezifuywayo zabantu abadumileyo kunye nendlela abakwazi ngayo ukuqhubeka nazo ngaphandle kokuphulukana nexesha namandla abo. Imithombo yamanzi ekati sisisombululo esiphezulu. Sinokujonga kwiiakhawunti ezininzi ze-Instagram zabantu abadumileyo, njengoMarthe Stewart kunye noJay Z, abathanda iikati zabo kwaye banqwenelela olona nyango lungcono. Masiqwalasele lo mba untununtunu kwaye sijonge ukuba kungathetha ntoni kuthi ukuthenga imithombo yamanzi ekati ukwenza ukuphila kweekati zethu kube lula kwaye kumnandi.

Abantu abadumileyo bashiya ikhaya rhoqo kwaye bafuna ukujongwa kwekati

Abantu abadumileyo bahlala befuna ukuba umthombo wamanzi kwiikati njengoko bekholelwa ukuba sesona sisombululo sokugcina ukuziphatha kwabo kukhangelwe kwaye kurekhodishwe. Le mithombo yamanzi efana naleyo ibonelelwe yinkampani ye-UAHPet yanele ukunika i-hydration efanelekileyo kwiikati kwaye uqinisekise ukuba unokulandela umkhondo wokuthatha kwawo amanzi ukude. Oku kubaluleke kakhulu kubantu abadumileyo abashiya amakhaya abo kakhulu kwaye kufuneka bahlale begadiwe impilo kunye nokuphila kweekati zabo. Ezi zixhobo zifumana amanzi ngokufunwa kweekati, isihluzo sikumgangatho we-premium kwaye sibanika ithuba lokuvuya kunye nokuzonwabisa ngelixa iinkosi zabo zixakeke yimisebenzi yazo yemihla ngemihla.

Imithombo yamanzi ekati kulula ukuyifumana 

Namhlanje akukho mntu unesizathu esivakalayo sokungayifumani imithombo yamanzi ekati ukuodola kwi-intanethi kwiikati zabo. Ukwenza uphendlo olulula lukaGoogle kuya kukubonisa ubuninzi beemodeli kwi-intanethi. Nangona kunjalo, kuya kufuneka uqiniseke ukuba ufumana ezo zixhotyiswe ngebhetri kwaye unoqhagamshelo olungcono olungenazingcingo kwinethiwekhi yakho yasekhaya. Kuyafuneka ukuba udibane ngqo nomthombo kwaye ufumane naluphi na ulwazi olungenazingcingo oludingayo malunga nobuninzi bamanzi iikati zakho ezisetyenzisiweyo ukuza kuthi ga ngoku kwaye utshintshe imibala yokukhanya ye-LED ukuze bazive bengcono xa ucinga ukuba badakumbile kukungabikho kwakho.

Imithombo yamanzi ekati yanamhlanje nayo ifuna ukulungiswa okuncinci kuneemodeli ezindala. Asikokubaxa xa sisithi imithombo yamanzi ekati ekhoyo ngoku ekhoyo kwimarike ayilulanga ukuba ixinzelelene njengoko yenziwe ngentsimbi engatyiwayo yezinto ezikumgangatho ophezulu zeplastiki. Kuthatha kuphela ukosula kunye nesepha ukuze zicoceke ngokwaneleyo kwiiseshoni zokunkcenkceshela ezilandelayo. Iikati zakho ziya kuyithanda into yokuba imithombo yazo yamanzi ibonakala icocekile kwaye icocekile ngaphandle kwesidingo sokuba khona yonke imihla kwaye ibacocele yona. Le mithombo ikwafuna ukutshintshwa kweebhetri kanye ngeentsuku ezili-120, nto leyo eyenzeka kathathu kuphela ngonyaka. Sicinga ukuba ungayifeza ngokulula loo nto ngokuyibeka kwikhalenda yakho.

Abantu abadumileyo njengeenketho ezingenazingcingo zeeModeli zoMthombo omtsha

Iinketho ezingenazintambo zibaluleke kakhulu, ngakumbi kubantu abadumileyo. Banesidingo sokuqhagamshelwa kwiikati zabo ngamaxesha onke njengoDave Franco onqabile ukungena esiteji ngaphandle kokujonga iikati zakhe ngaphambili. Le mowudi engenazingcingo ikuvumela ukuba ujonge inqanaba lamanzi kwaye uqinisekise ukuba basela amanzi aneleyo. Imithombo ikhona ukwenza ubomi bakho bube lula kwaye ikunike isiqinisekiso ukuba iikati zakho ziya kuhlala zikwimo yazo intle kwaye zinemomo eya kuzivumela ukuba zonwabe xa uzibandakanya nazo.

Indlela eNtsha iyalela iMithombo yaManzi yeKati yokuPhuculwa kweKhaya

Ukuphuculwa kwekhaya yeyona nto iphambili kubantu abadumileyo kunye nabo bonke abanye bethu. Imithombo yamanzi ekati iza ngemibala emininzi kunye neemilo onokuthi ukhethe kuzo. Banokuphucula i-aesthetics yekhaya lakho ngokulula kakhulu kunalo naluphi na ungenelelo olunokubakho. Ngenxa yeso sizathu, imithombo yamanzi ekati ibe yinto enye yokuncedisa abanini beekati abafuna ukuphucula ikhaya labo kwaye kwangaxeshanye benza iikati zabo zive ngcono kwaye zikhuselekile ngelixa bodwa ekhaya.

Amazwi okugqibela

Eyaziwayo okanye akunjalo, abanini beekati bafuna ukunika unyango olungcono kwiikati zabo. Yinto wonke umntu ayaziyo kwaye uyayiqonda kuba ezi zincedisi zifumaneka lula kwi-intanethi kwaye zibiza iqhezu lexabiso lazo lokuqala xa kuthelekiswa neminyaka embalwa eyadlulayo xa zazivela kuqala. Ukuzinika iikati zakho kuya kuphucula ubudlelwane bakho kunye nabo kwaye kubenze bazive bebahle kwaye benamandla kwakhona nangona bebambekile ekhaya iiyure ezininzi.
