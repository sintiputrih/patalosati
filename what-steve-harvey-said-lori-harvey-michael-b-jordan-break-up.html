What Steve Harvey Said About Lori Harvey And Michael B. Jordan Break Up

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, February 19, 2024

Lori Harvey and Black Panther star Michael B. Jordan were undoubtedly one of pop culture’s most adorable pairings. Since their relationship became public knowledge in 2020, the two have displayed nothing but unwavering devotion to each other, leaving fans confident that they were destined for the long haul. Tragically, Lori and Jordan decided to end their relationship early this month, after an exciting year and half-long run.Lori’s father, Steve Harvey, confirmed the news on an episode of The Steve Harvey Morning Show, before rendering some forthright comments and lighthearted quips about the break-up. 8 Why Did Michael B. Jordan And Lori Harvey Break Up?

Lori Harvey and Michael B. Jordan were one of those celebrity couplings that seemed destined to last a lifetime. While the two have not publicly commented on their break-up, a source close has revealed to People that the split had a lot to do with commitment issues.

According to the source, Lori “wasn’t ready to commit. [She] is very focused on her career. [She] realized that they weren’t on the same page [while she and Jordan] were making plans for their future.”

7 Steve Harvey Supported Michael B. Jordan And Lori Harvey’s Relationship

Despite being somewhat uncomfortable with Lori and Jordan’s PDA, Steve Harvey was incredibly supportive of their relationship.

At the start of their romance, the Family Feud host commented on the relationship saying, “I like this one. I still got my eye on him. I mean, I like him, but like I say to all of ’em, ‘I might like you, I might approve of you, but I got a thumb-sized section of my heart filled with nothing but pure hatred for your ass. Just in case I need it.’”

6 Steve Harvey Has Made Peace With Michael B. Jordan And Lori Harvey’s Break Up

Many avid fans were heartbroken by Lori and Michael B. Jordan’s unexpected break-up. However, Steve Harvey does not appear to share their sentiments.

While confirming the news of their break-up on his talk show, Steve Harvey expressed his commitment to moving forward despite the unfortunate ending. The comedian told the eager audience, "I feel fine. I'm fine...I still gotta go to work...I still gotta take care of my family."

5 Steve Harvey Still Thinks Michael B. Jordan Is A Cool Guy

Back in March 2021, Steve Harvey appeared on TheEllen DeGeneres Show, where he gushed over Micheal B. Jordan saying, “This guy is such a good guy, man. He’s one of the nicest guys. I’ve met his father. I’ve sat up with him, we’ve talked for hours [and] I just can’t find nothing wrong with him.”

Despite the break-up, the comedian has not lost his respect or admiration for Jordan and still thinks of him as a “cool guy.”

4 Steve Harvey Is Glad Michael B. Jordan And Lori Harvey Can Still Be Friends

Michael B. Jordan and Lori Harvey’s break-up was, by all indications, mutually agreed upon and peaceful. Unsurprisingly, Steve Harvey is grateful that things did not take a turn for the worst despite the intense emotions involved.

The father of seven told The Steve Harvey Morning Show crowd, “As long as everybody can walk away in peace, be friends. I ain’t heard nobody say they busted no windows or nothing. As long as you don’t put your hands on my daughter, I don’t give a damn what you do.”

3 Steve Harvey Understands And Supports His Daughter

Lori Harvey and Michael B. Jordan’s abrupt break-up sparked rumors that Lori might have cheated on the Creed star. Despite rampant criticism and speculation, Steve Harvey is committed to supporting and understanding his daughter.

In his words, “I’m team Lori, 1000 percent. She’s my daughter. I love her, I support her… Things happen. It's hard to be young and in the limelight and have a successful relationship.”

2 What Has Steve Harvey Learned From Michael B. Jordan And Lori Harvey’s Break Up?

Lori Harvey and Michael B. Jordan’s break-up has seemingly taught Steve Harvey an important lesson on relationships.

After rendering some serious comments on the break-up, the 65-year-old comedian took time to reflect on his own relationship history. “I just wish I could have broke up without the cost factor! I got to start learning from my children, get out early! I waited way too late.”

1 Steve Harvey Thinks That Lori Harvey And Michael B. Jordan Will Be Just Fine

Sources close to Michael B. Jordan and Lori Harvey claim that while the two are trying to move on, “they’re both heartbroken and upset” and “still love each other.”

While Lori and Jordan are still reeling from their break-up, Steve Harvey is confident that things will fall into place in the long run. In his words, “It’s a breakup. I’m pretty sure they’ll be fine. People break up all the time.”
