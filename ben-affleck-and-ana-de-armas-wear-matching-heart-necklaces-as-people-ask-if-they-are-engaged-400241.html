Ben Affleck And Ana De Armas Wear Matching Heart Necklaces As People Ask If They Are Engaged

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 5, 2024

Ben Affleck and Ana de Armas went viral this weekend after photos of the couple with Ben’s children with ex-wife Jennifer Garner made their social media rounds. According to Suzy Kerr, the couple has gone official with their relationship now that Ana has been introduced to the kids. It’s a milestone for the Deep Water stars who reportedly fell in love during filming their upcoming movie. Fans are thrilled for Ben as it seems his relationship with Ana is the real deal. They are also said to be self-quarantining together and past photos showed Jennifer Garner picking up and dropping off her children without Ana being around. It looks like Ben took his time before introducing the children to Ana.

Now that the children have met Ben and things are getting official and serious, some are questioning if Ben and Ana are engaged. Ana has been spotted wearing a large, ruby ring and there are rumors that suggest it is a temporary placeholder as Ben Affleck plans to let Ana pick out her own diamond sparkler.

Also going viral are photos of Ben and Ana wearing what appears to be matching heart necklaces. It seems these two are ready to signify their relationship with statement jewelry making the reports about a post-Coronvirus engagement ring more plausible.

It’s been quite a while since Ben Affleck seemed to be as serious about someone as he is Ana. Though he has had plenty of girlfriends since his split with Jennifer Garner, none of the relationships proved to be helpful to his sobriety. Ben has had his fair share of struggles and people are happy to see him with someone who is encouraging him to be the best he can be and also brings out the best in him.

So far, the consensus is that Ben and Ana have a healthy relationship and fans hope things stay as positive as they appear right now.
