The Diplomat: the cast of the Netflix show and where you've seen them before

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, April 5, 2024

Stars of the drama include Keri Russell and Rufus Sewell

    The Diplomat is Netflix's freshest show that is gripping viewers with its plot and brilliant cast. The eight-part series tells the story of Kate Wyler, a high-profile diplomat who moves her life across the pond to work at the heart of the British government.

    But as well as dealing with an intense job on the international stage, Kate finds herself also juggling her troubled marriage to a fellow diplomat.
