Kylie Jenner Wears Orange Naked Dress with Shoulder Pads by Julien MacDonald

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, March 5, 2024
  • Kylie Jenner wore a sheer orange naked dress with major cutouts by Julien Macdonald for dinner in L.A.
  • The beauty mogul flaunted the look, which also includes a pair of razor-sharp shoulder pads, on Instagram late last night.
  • Other Kardashian-Jenner sisters have also worn the brand before.

Kylie Jenner ended Memorial Day Weekend dressed as the real-life embodiment of the fire emoji. The 21-year-old beauty mogul stepped out in a super sultry (and super sheer) orange look by Julien Macdonald for dinner in Los Angeles on Monday night—and you know she had to flaunt the look on Instagram too.

The sheer frock was covered in citrus-hue beads and featured a plunging neckline, side and back cutouts, and a pair of intense shoulder pads. The dress is currently available to shop on LUISAVIAROMA for $16,917, but for billionaire Kylie Jenner, that's probably nothing.

According to photos from her outing, Jenner wore pointed PVC heels from her brother-in-law Kanye West's brand, Yeezy.

Yeezy Transparent pumps (SEASON 8)

Macdonald, who favors sexy designs with sheer fabrics, risqué cutouts, and revealing silhouettes, is clearly a favorite of the Kardashian-Jenner clan. In December, Kylie's sister Kendall Jenner (also a naked dress enthusiast) sported a see-through long-sleeved gown by the designer to the British Fashion Awards. The 23-year-old model wore nothing under the near-translucent look but tiny nude underwear.

Samir Hussein//Getty Images

Kendall Jenner attends the 2018 British Fashion Awards at London’s Royal Albert Hall.

And back in 2015, big sister Kim Kardashian wore a black, skin-tight jumpsuit by Macdonald featuring various woven cutouts along the sleeves and bodice. The look was right up Kim K's alley, and she completed the ensemble with strappy sandals and a thin black choker.
