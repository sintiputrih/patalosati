Sky Field (Japanese: スカイフィールド Sky Field) is a Stadium card. It was first released as part of the Roaring Skies expansion.

Card text

Each player can have 8 Pokémon on his or her Bench.

(When this card leaves play, each player discards Benched Pokémon until he or she has 5 Pokémon on the Bench. The owner of this card discards first.)

This card stays in play when you play it. Discard this card if another Stadium card comes into play. If another card with the same name is in play, you can't play this card.

Release information

This card was included in the Roaring Skies expansion, first released in the Japanese Emerald Break expansion. It was reprinted as a Mirror Reverse Holofoil in the Japanese Premium Champion Pack. In Japan, it was reprinted in The Best of XY subset, Ultra Sun Deck Build Box, and the Extra Regulation Box. It was also reprinted as one of the SV-P Promotional cards for the Extra Battle Day event on January 1st, 2024, and its stamp is shown on the card.
