RaeLynn Brings RaVe Tour to Nashvilles Mercy Lounge Celeb Secrets Country

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, July 25, 2024

RaeLynn took her popular RaVe Tour to the next level with a SOLD OUT headline show at Nashville’s Mercy Lounge last night (May 24).

The 23-year-old “Wildhorse” singer kicked the night off with a Radio Disney Country-curated playlist to pump up crowd of fans, friends, family and industry insiders before her performance.

RaeLynn’s energetic set included songs from her No. 1 debut album, WILDHORSE, and an unreleased new track “Magic.” Highlights of the show included her impeccable rapping skills and when young members of the audience joined her onstage to sing GOLD-certified hit “God Made Girls.” She ended the special night by joining her fans for a RaVe-style dance party.

“Performing in Nashville can be a scary thing because the city is filled with such amazing music and artists. To find out the show sold out right before I took the stage meant the world to me,” shared RaeLynn. “My fans came to party and sing their hearts out. They knew every word to every song and danced with me ‘til the very end! My RaVe Tour shows have showed me how amazing my fanbase is and how much they love my debut album!”
