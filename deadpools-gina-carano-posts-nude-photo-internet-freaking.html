The Internet Is Freaking Out Over Deadpool Actress Gina Caranos Nude Photo

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, April 24, 2024

It seems Gina Carano has got fans in a frenzy thanks to a nude photo she shared on her Instagram account over the weekend.

Recommended Videos

The mixed martial artist turned movie star has shown up in some pretty big blockbusters like Fast & Furious 6 and Deadpool, though some of you may also know her for her starring role in the 2011 pic Haywire. Or perhaps you’re just familiar with her from her MMA career.

Whatever the case, though the pic was quickly taken down, it soon found its way onto Twitter, where users have been freaking out over the whole thing – with some reacting positively and some, well, not so much. In fact, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu legend Renzo Gracie was just one of many who scolded her, and you can see what he had to say down below:

Of course, there are a lot – and we do mean a lot – more reactions where those came from and with tons of Tweets still pouring in on the topic, it seems to be all anyone can talk about right now. But looking ahead and what’s to come for Gina Carano, well, we’ll soon see her return in one of television’s hottest properties, The Mandalorian.

The actress will be back as Cara Dune in season 2 of the hit Disney Plus series, which arrives on the streaming platform this fall. And beyond that? Well, she’s got no further credits listed on IMDb as of yet, but given how impressive her career on both the big and small screen has been thus far, we can’t imagine it’ll be too long before Gina Carano finds her next project.
