Meet Victoria Zito! Her Bio, Age, Family, Boyfriend, Height

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 10, 2024

Bravo’s new reality series, Real Girlfriends in Paris!, which kicked off on the network on September 5th 2022, follows six young ex-pats who have moved from the States to live and work in Paris. Anya Firestone, Emily Gorelik, Margaux Lignel, Adja Toure, Victoria Zito, and Kacey Margo are the six stars of Real Girlfriends in Paris!

In this article, we shall discuss Victoria Zito’s age, boyfriend, job, net worth, and more.

Victoria Zito On Bravo’s RGIP (Real Girls In Paris)

Unlike Emily in Paris, Bravo’s RGIP does not glamorize and romanticize the Parisian lifestyle. Instead, the reality series will showcase all the highs and lows in the life of the cast as they adjust to their respective situations in front of the cameras.

Also, since they are miles away from their families, the stars of the show must support each other while all of them navigate job obstacles, dating woes, and cultural shocks.

RGIP star Victoria has been living in Paris for 5 years now. She moved to Paris to pursue a career in the fashion industry. In the show, we might see Victoria reach new career milestones and date several men.

Victoria’s best friend Yoanne Mobengo will also make an appearance in the new Bravo show. Yoanne is a popular fashion stylist and has worked with several A-list celebrities already. He has more than 212 thousand followers on his @leyoanne Instagram account.

Moreover, it has already been hinted by Victoria herself that there might be a fight between the two in front of the cameras.

Victoria Zito Age

On April 11, 2021, Victoria Zito made an Instagram post announcing that she’d turned 25 years old. That means Victoria’s age, as of this writing, was 26 and her birth year is 1996.

Does Victoria Zito Have A Boyfriend?

In the past, Victoria Zito has many times tweeted about marriage, love, and relationship in general. One time, she tweeted: ‘Can’t wait to be married one day and come home to lots of flowers from my husband! Ahhhh :).’ The other time, she wrote, “If you and your significant other aren’t constantly blowing each other’s DMs up w memes, are you even dating?”

According to Victoria’s bio on BravoTV, she was recently divorced. Any information about Victoria’s ex-husband is unavailable though.

RGIP star Victoria was single coming to the show. Now, however, she is all ready to start a new chapter in her life and explore all the romantic opportunities in front of the cameras.

Victoria Zito Job

In the US, Victoria Zito worked as the Production Manager and Stylist for Kona Body. Soon, she started working for Firefly Trend as a social media and digital marketing consultant, merchandising intern, and assistant buyer.

Passionate about fashion, Victoria joined El Centro College and started studying passion. After she received a scholarship to study at Parsons in 2017, she moved to Paris. There, she studied fashion/apparel design and also did a Traditional Haute Couture Embroidery course in 2018 at the Ecole Maison Lesage institute.

From August 2019 to March 2022, Victoria worked as a Head Designer for Victoria Zito x Marciante and Co collaboration for Marciante and Company.

According to her LinkedIn handle, Victoria is currently the head designer at a fashion company called Chloe Colette. She also works as a production runner for Indigital and JJ Productions.

How Much Is Victoria Zito Net Worth?

Reportedly, Victoria Zito’s net worth was estimated to be above $1 million as of September 2022.

Victoria Zito Height

Victoria Zito stands rather short at a height of 5 feet 4 inches.

Recently, on one of her Instagram stories, Victoria talked about her experiences with plastic surgery. She shared a picture of a minor bruise on her face along with mild soreness.

Victoria also advised the ones who have had plastic surgeries to come forth and open up about their experiences with it. By being open about plastic surgery, Victoria believes young women will feel empowered and it will also help them choose what they want for themselves.

In another story, the Real Girlfriends In Paris star Victoria revealed her own surgeries, which include lip fillers, cheekbone fillers, and chin fillers.

Victoria Zito Family

Talking about Victoria Zito’s family, her mother’s name is Terrie Zito and she was 63 years old in 2022. According to Terrie’s Facebook, she is a former sales agent at Elegant Additions and a former sales/designer at Floor and Decor.

Victoria is close to her mother and on February 26, 2015, she posted a collage containing pictures of her mother and herself, wishing her mother the happiest of birthdays.

Also, according to Bravo’s official website, Victoria’s mother always encouraged her daughter to stay close to home and stick to what she knows.

Any information about Victoria’s father, or Terrie’s husband, was unavailable as of this writing.

In the Relationship section of Facebook, Victoria’s mother has written “It’s complicated,” which could be a possible indication of Victoria’s parents’ separation. However, there are no confirmations yet.

The reality TV star also has a younger brother, Carson, who suffers from autism. Victoria has featured her brother on her Instagram many times.

Some of Victoria’s other family members are Dave Pearson, Russel Pickett, Chery Brock Arrington, Amber Scherer, and Logan Sams.
