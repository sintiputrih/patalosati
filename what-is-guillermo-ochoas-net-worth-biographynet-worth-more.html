What is Guillermo Ochoas Net Worth: Biography,Net Worth &amp; more

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, October 1, 2024

Guillermo Ochoa is a Mexican professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Serie A club Salernitana. Memo as he his popularly called is also the skipper of the Mexican national team. He has an estimated net worth of US$5 million as of July 2023.

His primary source of income comes from his football career, thus salaries, bonuses and endorsement deals with brands and companies. He has always invested in a number businesses and technology start-ups that will in the near future further increase his net worth.

Who is Guillermo Ochoa?

Francisco Guillermo Ochoa Magaña, born July 13, 1985 in Guadalajara, Jalisco, Mexico. He is a Mexican national and currently 38 years of age. He has been nicknamed “Memo,” a Hispanic with a zodiac sign, Cancer. Information about his parents, siblings, early children and educational background are unknown.

Ochoa began playing professional football when he was 18 years with Club América in 2004. He won the 2005 Campeón de Campeones and the 2006 CONCACAF Champions’ Cup with América. He claimed the Golden Glove awards for the 2006–07 season.

He made more than 200 appearances for the club and was the first choice goalkeeper until 2011 when he was transferred to Ligue 1 club Ajaccio in France.

At Ajaccio, he played three seasons up until the club were relegated from the Ligue 1. Another team came calling for his signature and that was Spanish giants, Málaga. He went on to further in the first team but failed to make himself a mainstay in the team after being there for two seasons.

In the summer of 2016, his parent club Málaga sent him on a season long loan at Granada. At the end of the season, he did impress his loan club nor was in the plans of his parent club so he decided to move on, signing for Standard Liege in July 2017. Two seasons with his current club and he returned to Club América.

Guillermo Ochoa is married to an established Mexican fashion model called Karla Mora. The couple had long been dating since 2009 before getting married in Spain on July 28, 2017. Ochoa and Mora have been blessed with three children; two daughters and a son Guillermo Ochoa.

How many houses and cars does Guillermo Ochoa have?

Memo lives in a luxurious US$1.9 million mansion with his family in Mexico City. The property comes with several features such as a swimming pool, water fountains, trees, shrubs and a huge garden.

The goalkeeper also owns a number of luxurious cars including the Tesla Model X and a Mercedes-Benz among a few others.

How much does Guillermo Ochoa make per year?

Guillermo Ochoa’s contract with Liga MX side CF America saw his pocket US$4.4 million annually. That figure made him the highest paid Mexican player on the team.

What investments does Guillermo Ochoa have?

Memo Ochoa comes from a lineage of business-minded people and that has had a great impact on him personally. To that, he has been down to taking bold steps to secure his future by investing in several companies such as Kavak, Altered Ventures, and two technology start-ups Trebel Y troquer.

Kavak is a company dedicated to trading on-line of pre-owned and used cars. Other shareholders are soccer player Katty ‘Killer’ Martinez, and Mexican pilot Sergio ‘Checo’ Perez.

Altered Ventures is a venture company that is specialized in videogames, eSports and VR/AR. He made a US$1 million joint investment towards a hybrid strategy between public and private markets.

How many Endorsement deals does Guillermo Ochoa have?

The Mexican international goalkeeper has made several endorsement deals with some of the biggest brands all through his career to this day. Among these brands are Coca Cola, Vic, Nike, Michelob Ultra, Bimbo, Xbox, Hugo Boss and Expedia among others.

How many Philanthropy works has Guillermo Ochoa supported?

Guillermo Ochoa is a philanthropist by all standards. He has shown love and compassion towards those in need specifically to children. Although not much has been documented about his generosity to others, he has been reported by several outlets to have a soft spot for charitable works.

One notable instance was when he donated his prize money to Granada’ Team Foundation after being voted the best player of the 2016/17 season.
