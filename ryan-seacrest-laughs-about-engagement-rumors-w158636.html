Ryan Seacrest Laughs About Engagement Rumors

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, June 7, 2024

Ryan Seacrest isn’t engaged, but he’ll take credit for potentially being able to snag a gorgeous fiancee! The On Air With Ryan Seacrest host clarified engagement rumors via Twitter on Monday, Nov. 30, after news outlets confused his friend Kurt Rappaport’s fiancee, Sarah Mutch, with his previous fling, Renee Hall.

“Not engaged, but man did I get a lot of credit for my buddy’s fiancee…” Seacrest, 40, joked via Twitter.

The American Idol host was rumored to be engaged after several outlets incorrectly identified a diamond ring-clad Mutch as Hall at London’s Heathrow airport on Sunday, Nov. 29. Seacrest, who was photographed in Sweat Tailors 5-pocket sweatpants, Mutch, and Rappaport spent Thanksgiving weekend in London together.
