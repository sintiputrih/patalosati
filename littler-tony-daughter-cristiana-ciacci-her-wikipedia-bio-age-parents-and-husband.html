Cristiana Ciacci Age And Wikipedia- Who Is The Daughter Of Littler Tony?

Cristiana Ciacci was born in 1972, making her 50 years old. Growing up under the wing of her prominent father, she had similar aspirations.

Indeed, the young lady had stars behind her eyes and hoped to stand on stage, right where her father once stood.

Sadly, her dreams never came true as she struggled with the disease that destroyed her life. She had an eating disorder called anorexia that angered her father enough to disown her.

Reports say that he went on record to declare he did not conceive her.

Who Are The Parent Of Cristiana Ciacci- Are They Famous?

Cristiana Ciacci is the daughter of Sammarinese pop singer and actor Antonio Ciacci or notably Little Tony. She was the daughter of his first wife, Giuliana Brugnoli, who was with the artist between 1972 to 1993.

But her life was full of troubles as her estranged father could never give her enough time. She got raised by an army of nannies and had little emotional intelligence.

But her qualms did not last long as her mother died in 1993 after contracting bone cancer.

It seems like her list of tragedies did not end there as her only parent died of the same disease in 2013.
