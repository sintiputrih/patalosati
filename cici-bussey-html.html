Cici Bussey - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

Cousin of Robert Kardashian known as part of the Kardashian family's inner circle. She made her first appearance on Keeping Up with the Kardashians in its second season. She managed the Kardashian Khaos store in The Mirage in Las Vegas from 2011 to 2014. She joined the family when they competed on Celebrity Family Feud in 2018.

Before Fame

After Kris Jenner and Robert Kardashian separated in the 1990s, she remained close to Kris and their children.

Trivia

At age 40, she was diagnosed with non-Hodgkin lymphoma. She was treated with a stem cell transplant and chemotherapy. In 2021, Kim Kardashian posted on Instagram that Bussey was officially cancer free after a decades-long battle.

Family Life

Her mother and Robert Kardashian's mother were sisters.

Associated With

On Keeping Up with the Kardashians, she warned the family about Scott Disick's alleged infidelity in 2015. She later encouraged Kourtney Kardashian to stay home with her children instead of going to Egypt with her boyfriend Younes Bendjima.
