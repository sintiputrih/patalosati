The Left Poké Ball (Japanese: のこされたボール Remaining Ball) is a Key Item introduced in Generation VII. It is involved in a minor side-quest in Pokémon Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon.

Effect

This Poké Ball contains a late old man's Dartrix. When given to his granddaughter, a female Preschooler in northern Malie City, she will give the player a Mental Herb as thanks.
