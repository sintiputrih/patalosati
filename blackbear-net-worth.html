Blackbear: Career

Early Career

Matthew started his career early in high school. He was a member of a Florida-based rock band named “Polaroid“.

Leakmob records signed the band when he was in freshman year of high school.

Polaroid became increasingly popular over time. Meanwhile, in 2008 the singer had already started writing solo music.

Eventually, he left the band and moved to Atlanta to start a new career. He collaborated with the singer/songwriter Ne-Yo.

His solo career started with an EP entitled brightness in 2008 and it was followed by 2009’s Contrast and 2010’s Exposure.

In 2010 and 2011, the artist released several EPs solo and in collaboration with other musicians.

Only in the fall of 2011, Matthew decided to take on the stage name Blackbear.

Moreover, in early 2012, Blackbear co-wrote Justin Bieber’s hit single “Boyfriend“. The song went on to be a number two hit on the Billboard Hot 100.

He then changed lanes to pursue R&B music, hence released Foreplay on April 20.

Blackbear then released his debut mixtape Sex on October 31, 2012. Meanwhile, Matthew also appeared on MGK‘s single End of the Road.

In April of 2014, he released an EP, The Afterglow, which took him to number four on Billboard’s Uncharted listing of releases from upcoming artists.

He was one of the first musicians to monetize streaming music on Soundcloud.

In February of 2015, he released the first full-length album of his career, Deadroses.

The single, “idfc“, was on Billboard‘s R&B Hot 100 chart. It also held a spot on the Billboard R&B charts for more than a year.

The album was also financially very successful.

Recent Career

2016 was a good year for the artist because he collaborated with popular fellow musicians like Mike Posner, American rock band Linkin Park, Jacob Sartorius,  P-Lo, and Phoebe Ryan.

From 2017 to 2020, he has released three studio albums, namely, Digital Druglord (2017), Anonymous (2019), Everything Means Nothing (2020).

Lastly, on June 4, 2021, Balckbear released the album “U love U” with Canadian musician Tate McRae. The artist is heavily promoting the brand and also has merch available for it on his website.
