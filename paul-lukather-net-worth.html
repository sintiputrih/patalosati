Paul Lukather Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Paul Lukather (born 1926) is an actor and voice-over artist who is mostly known as giving voice-overs for many video games including "Legacy of Kain" series where he voiced Vorador. He also had a role in Metal Gear Solid 2: Sons of Liberty where he did the English voice-over for President James Johnson. Lukather made dozens of television appearances on Perry Mason, The Fugitive, The F.B.I., Mission: Impossible, Cannon, and many others. In 1966 he guest starred on Perry Mason as defendant 'Duke' Maronek in "The Case of the Misguided Model." ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
