Alessandra Ambrosio Hangs Out with Her Adorable Look-Alike Kids in New Instagram

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, March 14, 2024

The apple definitely doesn't fall far from the tree in this family.

Alessandra Ambrosio took to Instagram today to share a photo of her and her two look-alike kids, and we can't believe how much the cuties resemble their famous mom. In the snap, the 35-year-old poses for a selfie with her 7-year-old daughter Anja Louise and 4-year-old son Noah Phoenix. The supermodel and Anja both smile for the camera, while Noah cutely scrunches up his nose. The group appears to be enjoying the warm California weather by taking a dip in the swimming pool.

"My Little Squad

Minha turminha !!! #mondaymotivation #calilife #summer2016 #foreveronvacation," the proud mom captioned the darling picture.

But this isn't the first time Ambrosio has posted sweet snaps of her children. The star returned to her home country of Brazil to carry the Olympic torch at the opening ceremony earlier this month, and she brought her little ones along to partake in the exciting festivities.

This is definitely one picture perfect squad.
