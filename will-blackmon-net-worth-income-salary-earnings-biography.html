Will Blackmon Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, February 27, 2024
In 1984, William Edwards Blackmon was born on October 27, 1984, in Jacksonville, Florida, and is a cornerback in the National Football League for the Jacksonville Jaguars (NFL). The Green Bay Packers selected him in the fourth round of the 2006 NFL Draft (115th overall) as part of their offensive line. Blackmon was born in Providence, Rhode Island, but grew up in the city of Cranston, the state’s largest city. Because his mother died of Crohn’s Disease when he was six years old, he has been raised by his father and grandmother. At Bishop Hendricken High School in Warwick, Rhode Island, where he participated in football and track, he was named All-American. As a senior at Hendricken, Blackmon was named to the USA Today, ESPN.com, SuperPrep, and PrepStar All-America first-team in 2001 as well as the All-America second team in 2002. At the conclusion of his senior season, he was awarded Gatorade Rhode Island Player of the Year. He was a member of the Boston Undergraduate Eagles football team during his college years.
