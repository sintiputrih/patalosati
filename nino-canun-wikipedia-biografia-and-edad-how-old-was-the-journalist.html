Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 10, 2024

One of Mexicans’ top journalists Nino Canún is dead and to shed light on his personality, we take a look at his Wikipedia, Biografia and Edad.

Mexican journalist Nino Canun, who was 82, passed suddenly on Wednesday, June 21, 2023. The radio and television personality who became well-known for the program in the 1990s passed away. The first person to learn about this via Twitter was journalist Sergio Sarmiento.

Canun began his career in 1981 as a newscaster for the now-defunct public television network Imevision and for multiple radio stations in Mexico City. In 1990, he established the business that would make him well-known. So what do you think?

The journalist posed a series of questions on the public’s opinions on numerous issues, which were broadcast for four years on Televisa networks. Canun discussed public insecurity one day, rock & roll’s place in the nation the next, and concluded the week with a panel discussion on UFOs.

The driver continued to participate in numerous radio spaces after finding popularity on television, however, most recently he solely had his own spaces on social networks.

In January 2019, Canun made an appearance at President Andres Manuel Lopez Obrador’s morning press conference at the National Palace, where he claimed that the media had censored him during the administrations of Vicente Fox and Enrique Pena Nieto. After about four minutes of intervention, he praised the president’s work.

Nino Canún Wikipedia and Biografia

He had no Wikipedia page. That is quite surprising considering his high status as a journalist. Probably with his passing his family will dedicate a page on Wikipedia to make it easier for his fans to get relevant information about him, spanning from his career to his personal life.

Nino Canun Edad (age) was 82 at the time of his passing. He was born in Mexico to his parents, but Canun was also a private guy who kept every detail of his life away from the media.
