Who Plays Shane On 'Girlboss?' You've Seen Johnny Simmons Before

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 8, 2024

If you are one of the many people who spent the weekend marathoning Girlboss, you probably also spent that time switching between screens. When you weren't glued to the Netflix series on your laptop, tablet, or TV (yes, some people still watch television sets), you were likely using a secondary device to look up facts about the real life Sophia Amoruso and the actors on the show. But, if you forgot to look up the actor who plays Shane because you fell down a Wikipedia rabbit hole or got distracted by Instagram, I've got your back. So, who plays Shane on Girlboss?

The Netflix series loosely based on Nasty Gal founder Sophia Amoruso's autobiography features a hunky love interest for the TV portrayal of Sophia: Shane, played by Johnny Simmons. Though both Shane and Amoruso's real ex-husband Joel Jerek Degraff are musicians, Amoruso told Bustle that all of the characters are inventions for the show — aside from Sophia. So, since there's no real person Shane is based on, let's get to know the actor behind the man instead.
