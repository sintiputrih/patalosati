Who Is Dean Norris?, Age, Height, Weight, Career, Films, Family, Net Worth: Born on April 8, 1963, Dean Norris, also known as Dean Joseph Norris is an American actor.

He developed a passion for acting at a younger age and has been consistent throughout his career, becoming one of the most sought-after American actors.

Norris is best known for portraying DEA agent, Hank Schrader on the AMC series; Breaking Bad (2008–2013) and its spin-off Better Call Saul (2020).

He also played town councilman James “Big Jim” Rennie on the CBS series; Under the Dome (2013–2015), and played mob boss Clay “Uncle Daddy” Husser on the TNT series; Claws (2017–2022).

Norris has appeared in films such as Lethal Weapon 2, Hard to Kill, Total Recall, Terminator 2: Judgment Day, The Firm, Starship Troopers, The Cell, Little Miss Sunshine, Evan Almighty, and Sons of Liberty.

He has also starred in The Book of Henry, Death Wish, Scary Stories to Tell in the Dark, The Hustle, Death Wish, Beirut, The Book of Henry, Fist Fight, Remember, Small Time, The Counselor, and The Frozen Ground.

His other film credit includes; Get the Gringo, Prom, How Do You Know, How Do You Know, The Heartbreak Kid, The Heartbreak Kid, The Heartbreak Kid, The One, American Gun, 3 Strikes, Sonic Impact, The Negotiator, and Without Limits.

Throughout his career, Norris has acted in several movies and more than 100 different TV shows. His TV shows include; American Dreams, Boston Legal, Without A Trace, Dragnet, Medium, Over There, The West Wing, NCIS, Dragnel, and Tremos, among others.
