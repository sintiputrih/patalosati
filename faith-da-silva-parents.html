“It’s been a while since I forgave him. I’ve made peace with the fact that you know, the life I have is not normal.”

“We can always start over, I want to experience my father’s love and I know I can only get that from you. But I can’t rush everything, I’ll wait. When you are ready, when I am fully ready, and when God gives us the opportunity, I know that will be a life-changing moment in my life.”
