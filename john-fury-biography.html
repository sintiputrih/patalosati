Who is John Fury?

John Fury is a renowned Irish boxing cornerman who has undoubtedly made a name for himself in the world of professional boxing.

John Fury had a remarkable journey as a boxer and later, as a cornerman. He is also known as Gypsy. He holds Irish nationality.

John Fury- Professional Life, Careers

John Fury started his boxing career when he was just 16 years old, and at the time, he didn’t have any formal training. However, his natural skills and his father’s guidance were enough to set him on the right path.

John Fury went on to become a professional heavyweight boxer, amassing a record of 8-4-1, which included six knockouts.

Though not an outstanding career, Fury’s boxing journey was filled with some impressive performances and unforgettable moments.

After retiring from boxing, John Fury took on a new career as a boxing coach, passing on his knowledge of the sport to the younger generation.

In 2013, Fury played a pivotal role in his son Tyson Fury‘s corner in his historic fight against Wladimir Klitschko.

The fight saw Tyson Fury winning the WBA, IBF, and WBO titles, a fantastic achievement made possible by John’s exceptional cornering skills.
