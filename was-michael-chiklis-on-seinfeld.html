Was Michael Chiklis on Seinfeld?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, May 2, 2024

Michael Chiklis is an American actor, television director, television producer and musician.

Chiklis has starred in numerous films and television series including The Shield (2002-2008), Fantastic Four: Rise of the Silver Surfer (2007), No Ordinary Family (2010-2011), American Horror Story (2014-2015), Gotham (2015-2017), Murder (2018), Hubie Halloween (2020), Coyote (2021), Don’t Look Up (2021), Winning Time: The Rise of the Lakers Dynasty (2022), and many more.

In 2002, Chiklis won the Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Lead Actor in the Drama Series The Shield.

Was Michael Chiklis on Seinfeld?

Yes, Michael Chiklis was on Seinfeld.

Michael Chiklis portrayed Steve Pocatello on Seinfeld ( The Stranded) in 1991.

“The Stranded” is the 27th episode of the sitcom Seinfeld. It is the tenth episode of the show’s third season. It first aired on November 27, 1991. The episode was originally produced for Season 2 but was postponed because Larry David was dissatisfied with the episode; it was therefore advertised as a “lost” episode. It was also released in the first and second season sets instead of the third.
