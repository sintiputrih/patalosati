Cate Blanchett is an Australian actor. Regarded as one of the best performers of her generation, she is known for her versatile work across independent films, blockbusters, and stage.

Cate Blanchett age

Cate Blanchett bio

In the Victoria suburb of Ivanhoe on May 14, 1969, Catherine Elise Blanchett was born. Her American father, Robert DeWitt Blanchett Jr., a Texan native who served in the United States Navy as a Chief Petty Officer before becoming an advertising executive, and her Australian mother, June both worked in real estate development and education.

When Robert’s ship broke down in Melbourne, they eventually met. Blanchett’s father passed away from a heart attack when she was ten years old, leaving her mother to care for the household. With an older sibling and a younger sister, Blanchett is the second of three children. She has English, some Scottish, and distant French heritage.

She attended the University of Melbourne to study economics and fine arts but left after a year to explore the world. Blanchett was invited to appear as an extra as an American cheerleader in the 1990 Egyptian boxing movie Kaboria; desperate for cash, she agreed. She relocated to Sydney upon her return to Australia, registered at the National Institute of Dramatic Art (NIDA), and earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in 1992.

Where does Cate Blanchett come from?
