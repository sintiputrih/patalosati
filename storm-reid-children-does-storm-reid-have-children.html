Storm Reid Children: Does Storm Reid Have Children?: Storm Reid is an American actress born on July 1, 2003.

She developed a love for acting at a very younger age debuting with the television film A Cross To Bear in 2012

In 2013, Storm Reid starred as Emily in the historical drama film 12 Years a Slave and became a household name for her role in the superhero film Sleight which was released in 2016.

She appeared in the movie, A Wrinkle in Time (2018), for which she was nominated for the Teen Choice Award for Choice Fantasy Movie Actress and the NAACP Image Award for Outstanding Breakthrough Role in a Motion Picture.

Storm Reid has also starred in several other films and movies including the thriller film Don’t Let Go (2019), the critically-acclaimed Netflix miniseries When They See Us (2019), and the horror film The Invisible Man (2020).

She currently stars in the HBO drama series Euphoria (2019–present) and played a minor role in the superhero film The Suicide Squad (2021).

In 2020, Reid received a nomination for the BET YoungStars Award and has since starred in the mystery thriller Missing (2023). She also portrays Riley Abel in the HBO series The Last of Us (2023).

Storm Reid Children: Does Storm Reid Have Children?

As of February 2023, the 19-year-old actress, Storm Reid was not yet a mother. She does not have any biological or adopted children.
