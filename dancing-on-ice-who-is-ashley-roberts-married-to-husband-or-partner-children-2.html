Dancing On Ice: Who Is Ashley Roberts Married To? Husband or Partner &amp; Children

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, March 15, 2024

Ashley Roberts was dating Geovanni Pernince but is not married yet. Learn what happened to her love life.

Ashley Roberts was a judge on Dancing on Ice for the eighth and ninth series but for the 14 series, it has yet to be disclosed if she is returning as a judge.

Since John Barrowman would not appear on the panel for the 2022 competition, Oti Mabuse will be replacing him accompanied by Jayne Torvill and Christopher Dean, and Ashley Banjo.

The show Dancing On Ice season 14 began airing on ITV on 16 January 2022.

Dancing On Ice: Who Is Ashley Roberts Married To? Husband Or Partner

Ashley Roberts, the Dancing On Ice judge was dating Strictly professional Giovanni Pernice has not been married to a husband so far.

Moreover, the couple split in early 2020 and they themselves confirmed that they were no longer dating.

Ashley was a contestant of Strictly who then started dating Giovanni, despite not being partnered with each other on the show.

Giovanni is an Italian professional dancer who is known for the show Strictly Come Dancing.

The Italian Open Latin Dance Champion also holds the Guinness World Record for Jive kicks and flicks, as well as Charleston swivels.

Ashley was previously in a brief relationship with Declan Donnelly. In 2018, she told The Mirror: “Being single, I have set boundaries on the men I date.”

“Once I have separated I do not check their Instagram. It is brutal. It is very dangerous. I have learned the hard way. I don’t feel like I need to block. I unfollow them.”

Does Ashley Roberts Have Children?

Ashley Roberts, the American singer, dancer, and tv personality does not have any children of her own.

But some of her bandmates from The Pussycat Dolls did have kids of their own.

As a member of the pop R&B girl singing and dance group, they gave us so many hits to date.

Ashley quit the band in 2010 and has since become an institution on British TV. The runner-up on ”I’m a Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here!” in 2012 has also starred in ”The Jump”.

Furthermore, she also co-hosted “Ant & Dec’s Saturday Night Takeaway” from 2013 to 2016.

Ashley Roberts Net Worth And Earnings

Ashley Roberts estimated net worth is around £1.2 million dollars which is $1.6 million in the US.

Her earnings are largely attributed to her career as a singer, dancer, reality tv star, and host.

She released her debut solo studio album, Butterfly Effect in 2014 which was a huge success.

She traveled all around the world following guru Amma and took several dancing and acting classes.
