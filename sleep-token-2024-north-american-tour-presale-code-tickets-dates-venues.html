About Sleep Token

News: Established in 2016, Sleep Token is a progressive metal group hailing from the United Kingdom, recognized for cultivating a devoted fan base through their cryptic and mysterious persona. The individuals comprising the band, including their frontman referred to as Vessel, maintain a veil of anonymity by consistently wearing masks and cloaks. This secretive approach contributes to the fascination and curiosity surrounding Sleep Token, prompting parallels to well-known bands such as Ghost and Slipknot.

The History of Sleep Token

Sleep Token embarked on their musical journey in 2016, introducing themselves to the music scene with the single “Thread the Needle.” Their unconventional approach and genre-defying music quickly gained attention, and the release of their debut EP, “One,” solidified their presence in the industry, earning them a growing fan base and critical acclaim.

Despite facing challenges, such as alleged doxxing incidents threatening to reveal their anonymous members, Sleep Token persevered. Their captivating performances and unique sound continued to resonate with fans. Signing with labels like Basick Records and later Spinefarm Records further propelled them into the spotlight.

In 2019, Sleep Token made a significant impact with the release of their highly praised debut album, “Sundowning,” showcasing their seamless blending of various genres. The momentum continued with the release of “This Place Will Become Your Tomb” in 2021, establishing them as a formidable presence in the progressive metal scene. Their third album, “Take Me Back to Eden,” released in 2023, further expanded their fanbase.
