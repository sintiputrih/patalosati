Pascal Siakam has been able to grasp quite a resounding net worth from his professional basketball career. His earnings stand firm at $30 million with professional contract and salary adding to his economic stature.

Pascal Siakam is a Cameroonian professional basketball player who plays for the NBA side, Toronto Raptors. He previously played college basketball for New Mexico State University.

Pascal Siakam Bio & Family: Age, Ethnicity, Height, College

The 26-years-of-age basketball star was born on 2 April 1994 in Doula, Cameroon. He is the youngest among the six children of his parents Tchamo Siakam and Victorie Siakam. His father, Tchamo, previously worked for a local transit company and he was also the mayor of Makenene. He sadly died in a car crash in October 2014.

Pascal has three brothers and two sisters in his family. His brothers, James Siakam, Christian Siakam, and Boris Siakam all play basketball. Likewise, his two sisters are named Vanessa Siakam and Raissa Siakam. The power forward holds black ethnicity.

About 6 feet and 9 inches (2.06 meters) tall in height, Pascal attended God’s Academy, the high school in Lewisville, Texas. Later on, he joined New Mexico State University and played college basketball three seasons.

His NBA Career, Net Worth

Pascal Siakam was selected by the Toronto Raptors as the 27th overall pick in the 2015 NBA draft. Later on 2nd October the same year, he became the first rookie to start for the Raptors’ season-opener. Pascal scored a season-high 14 points to give his side a 128-84 victory over the Atlanta Hawks on 3 December 2016.

The young power forward went on to shine on the 2017-18 season and 2018-19 season with several awards and records to his name. In the season opener of the 2019-20 season, he recorded 34 points, 18 rebounds, 5 assists, and 1 block to give his side a 130-122 victory over the New Orleans Pelicans.

From his professional basketball career, Pascal bags a commanding net worth of $30 million.

Pascal’s Contract & Salary

Pascal first penned a rookie-scale contract with the Toronto Raptors on 9 July 2016. For the 2016-18 season, he earned a salary of $1,196,040. In the next 2017-18 and 2018-19 seasons, Pascal had the salary of $1,312,611 and $1,544,951 respectively.

As per the sources, the 26-year-old basketball player is currently leading a single and career-focused life. Besides, no previous dating history of the Raptors’ power forward has surfaced on the internet.
