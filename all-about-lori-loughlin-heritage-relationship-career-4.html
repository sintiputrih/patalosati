All about Lori Loughlin | Heritage, relationship, career

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, January 12, 2024

Lori Anne Loughlin, better known as Lori Loughlin, is an American model, producer and actress. She is best known for her roles as Rebecca Donaldson-Katsopolis on Full House (1988-1995) and Fuller House (2016-present) on Netflix.

Jennifer Shannon on Garage Sale Mystery, Jody Travis on The Edge of Night, Debbie Wilson on The CW’s 90210, Abigail Stanton on When Calls the Heart and Ava Gregory on Summerland on The WB are some of her other credits.

She began her modeling career at a young age, appearing in several advertisements before moving into the television and film industries. She has received multiple honors for her incredible performances and is currently filming “Fuller House,” the sequel to “Full House.”
