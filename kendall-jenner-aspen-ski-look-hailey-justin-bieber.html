Kendall Jenner Takes Aspen Ski Trip With Hailey and Justin Bieber

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, March 13, 2024

There’s no therapy like snow therapy, and no one knows that better than Kendall Jenner.

The supermodel headed to Aspen this week for a little wintertime getaway with pals Hailey and Justin Bieber. In photos captured by the Daily Mail, the 28-year-old was seen heading to breakfast Monday morning, wearing a white New Balance T-shirt over a long-sleeved undershirt, which she paired with matching white flared pants. To complete the cozy fit, Jenner added a pair of classic Ugg boots in chestnut brown.

BACKGRID

courtesy of Kendall Jenner via Instagram//Instagram

She was seen wearing this same ensemble in a video she uploaded to her Instagram Story later that day. Turns out she also hit the slopes with another pair of friends—Shaun White and Nina Dobrev. Former Olympic snowboarder White may have been giving Jenner some tips; she tagged him in a clip in which they were seen trying out some snowy ramps together.

courtesy of Kendall Jenner via Instagram//Instagram

This trip took place soon after Jenner’s split from musician Bad Bunny became public knowledge. An insider source told Entertainment Tonight: “Kendall and Bad Bunny have been doing their own things lately, and things slowly started to fizzle out between them.”

But it looks like Jenner has her friends by her side and enjoyed a much-needed getaway. She was also seen going out to dinner over the weekend with the Biebers. Hailey wore a long gray shearling coat from Ferragamo and black leather pointed-toe stiletto boots from Jimmy Choo. Meanwhile, Justin wore a black leather jacket over a white button-down with black slacks. For Jenner’s part, she wore a long collared coat featuring shaggy ivory fur over a black turtleneck and straight-leg pants.
