Ryan Hall Bio, Age, Job

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, October 9, 2024

The 2nd season of The Real Housewives of Salt Lake City is airing on Bravo, and one of the popular cast of RHOSLC, Lisa Barlow, has lately become the talk of the town. When Lisa mentioned marrying her sister’s boyfriend in one of the episodes of the Bravo show, the viewers of RHOSLC suddenly wanted to know every bit of information about Lisa’s sister, Genia Hall. Fans of the show were wondering if Lisa’s husband, John, and Genia dated in the past. However, Lisa herself cleared up the rumors through a tweet. Even though the discussion has been settled now, viewers still want to know about Lisa’s lookalike sister, Genia Hall. In this article, we will be talking about Genia Hall’s husband, Ryan Hall–from his personal life to age and job and more!

Meet Ryan Hall, [Lisa Barlow Sister] Genia Hall Husband

Everything started when Lisa said, “I married my sister’s boyfriend, but they didn’t sleep together,” on the show. Fans took no time to jump over Twitter to discuss the matter. One Twitter user wrote, “Omggg John Barlow dated Lisa’s sister before her… the familial ties of this franchise.”

When there was no sign of this discussion stopping anytime soon, Lisa herself clarified the matter through a tweet. She revealed that Lisa’s now-husband, John, and Genia only went on one date, and that too was not in a serious way.

Genia Hall is currently happily married to her husband, Ryan Hall. Although we don’t the story of how they met, or for how long they dated, we can confirm that they have been married for a long time. The lovely couple has four kids from their marriage. They are Ethan, Clark, Harrison, and Collier Hall and all of them live in Kansas at present with their parents.

From Genia’s Facebook, we can tell that Ethan celebrates his birthday on March 5th, Clark on March 29th, Collier on January 10th, and Harrison on April 12th.

One of Ryan and Genia’s sons, Ethan Hall, works at The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, whereas the other one, Harrison, was recently accepted into a pre-medical program.

Genia’s Facebook tells us how good of a bond the couple has in between them. On 2018 Father’s Day, Genia took to Facebook to write a sweet message to her love, Ryan. She wrote, “Happy Father’s Day to this incredible man! He is an amazing father and husband! So lucky to have found him! All of Ryan’s free time ( which isn’t much ) is spent on his family. He gives everything and anything he has to his family Happy Fathers Day Lovey! ( This is Ryan’s graduation photo… The guy was a total Babe)”

To talk a little about Ryan’s love, well we know that Genia studied at Brigham Young University and currently she works at She is Like Eve as the company’s CEO and creative director. As per the company’s website, “She is Like Eve is a line of luxury goods inspired to give women the confidence to live their lives and connect to their sensual side.

On She is Like Eve’s website, Genia has explained that she always had a passion for fashion. She revealed that she always used to attend fashion shows and explore the Garment District with her sisters as a child.

Ryan Hall Age

Ryan Hall was reportedly 47 years of age in 2022. Genia wished her husband a happy 45th birthday on Facebook on May 23, 2019.

Ryan Hall Job

According to Ryan Hall’s LinkedIn, he is a physician doctor by profession. Currently working at Via Christi Health as a general physician, Ryan graduated from Kansas City University with a master’s degree in Doctor of Medicine-MD in August 2003.

Ryan also received his education from Oklahoma State University (studied Emergency Medicine Residency) and Brigham Young University (studied Zoology/Animal Biology).

Besides Via Christi Health, Ryan also works at Fort Walton Beach Medical Center as an Emergency Medicine Physician and New Health Kansas as COVID Workplace Consultant.

Ryan’s past jobs include working at Stormont-Vail Health Care as an Emergency Medicine Physician and at Envision Healthcare as Providence Medical Center. He worked for those companies for more than 10 years, from 2010 to 2011.

As of this writing, we had no idea about Ryan’s salary or his net worth.
