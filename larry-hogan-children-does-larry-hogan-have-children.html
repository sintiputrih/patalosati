Hogan unsuccessfully campaigned for Maryland’s 5th congressional district in 1981 and 1992, the latter of which was incumbent Steny Hoyer’s closest race.

Hogan founded the Change Maryland organization in 2011, which he used to promote his 2014 gubernatorial campaign. He became governor in 2015 and was reelected in 2018.
