Wiki/Biography

Varun Bangera was born on 28 August in Mumbai, Maharashtra. His zodiac sign is Virgo. He did his graduation in international business from 2005 to 2008 at Carleton University, Ottawa, Canada. [1]

Career

He started his career at Cushman & Wakefield, a real estate services firm in the U.S., in the marketing and investments department in August 2008 and continued working there till May 2010. In 2010, he started his real estate company with the name VB Corp in Mumbai. He joined Pecan Partners, a consulting and business development firm in the U.S., in July 2013 as the managing director. He worked there for almost eight years. After quitting his job, he continued working for his own real estate business.
