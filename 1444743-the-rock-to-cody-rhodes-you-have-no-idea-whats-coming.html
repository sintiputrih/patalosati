The Rock To Cody Rhodes: You Have No Idea Whats Coming

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, May 13, 2024

The Rock has sent a defiant message to Cody Rhodes and all of the “Cody Crybabies” ahead of SmackDown this Friday night.

Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson may no longer be getting his Universal Title Match against Roman Reigns in April, but that doesn’t mean The Great One won’t be causing havoc for The American Nightmare on the road to WrestleMania.

The Rock took to Instagram earlier this morning to post a video of himself and Roman Reigns united walking out of the T-Mobile Arena last week. But many have seen that video before; what’s most important is the caption posted along with it. Stating the following:

“Hear the rumble
Hear the rumble
Run for dem life, when we step into the jungle

You f**ked around and found out quick, didn’t you boy?

You, your little friends and all your crybabies have no idea what’s coming.

The world’s on notice.

~ People’s Champ + Tribal Chief

#KillersInTheJungle
#Bloodline
#WrestleMania
@wwe @tkogrp @romanreigns,” The Rock said in a post.

What kind of chaos will The Rock and Roman Reigns cause this Friday night on SmackDown? We’ll find out soon enough.
