Ratchet &amp; Clank: Rift Apart Spybot locations

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, May 23, 2024

Ratchet & Clank: Rift Apart’s 10 Spybot collectibles are scattered across all nine levels. Collecting all 10 unlocks a special weapon at Ms. Zircon’s — the RYNO.

In this Rift Apart guide, we’ll show you where to find all nine Spybots. We’ve got maps and screenshots along with a short description for every one.

Corson V Nefarious City Spybot location

Head to the factory on the west side of the Corson IV Nefarious City. After navigating through to the roof, you’ll fight your way past several waves of Nefarious Troopers. At the end of all of the fighting, you’ll find the Spybot in the tunnel that leads back down to the factory entrance.

Sargasso Outpost L51 “Help Trudi” Spybot location

Once you can fly around with Trudi on Sargasso Outpost L51, start flying to her various perches to collect as many Zurpstones as you can. Once you collect 45 of them, Mort will call and tell you Trudi can also breathe fire. Use Trudi’s fire to shoot any of the Zurpstones that were out of reach before. Collect all 60 Zurpstones, and Mort will reward you with the Sargasso Spybot.

Scarstu Debris Field Zurkie’s Spybot locations

There are 2 Spybots to find in Scarstu Debris Field Zurkie’s. You cannot reach the first until after you’ve visited the Blizar Prime Blizon Mines.

Scarstu Debris Field Zurkie’s Spybot 1

Instead of heading through the rift into Zurkie’s, follow the path around the building to the right. There’s a Hookshot there that will launch you to this Spybot.

Scarstu Debris Field Zurkie’s Spybot 2

Complete the fourth stage of Zurkie’s Gold Cup, Vroom Around, to collect this Spybot.

Savali Urfdah Mesa Spybot location

In the northeast of Savali Urfdah Mesa, look for a series of platforms that lead to a tunnel. Follow that tunnel to find a wrecked pirate ship. You’ll find the Savali Spybot on the deck.

Blizar Prime Blizon Mines Spybot location

Find Chef Truli in the northeast of Blizar Prime Blizon Mines and help her defend her Vac-U-Suk machine. She’ll reward you with this Spybot. (If you ever get stuck during the waves, check along the back of the arena. Some of the enemies seem to get stuck in the lava there and stall your objectives.)

Torren IV Molonoth Gulch Spybot location

When you reach Torren IV Molonoth Gulch’s town early in the level, climb up and to the right. There’s a magnetic path that winds down to a lower level. Follow it down to find this Spybot.

Cordelion Kedaro Station Spybot location

In the room with Cordelion Kedaro Station’s Rubion Forge, look along a high ledge for a rift. Use it to pick up this Spybot.

Ardolis Pirate Base Spybot location

Grab a Sneedle from the north of Ardolis Pirate Base along the path to the Pirate Trials. Take it northeast, and you’ll find your way to an island where you’ll pick up this Spybot.

Viceron Zordoom Prison Spybot location

As you enter Viceron Zordoom Prison, you’ll make your way through some air ducts. You’ll find this Spybot behind a fan just off the main path.
